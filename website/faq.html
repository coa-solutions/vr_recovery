CLINICAL

Clinical & Safety

Evidence, safety protocols, and integration

This is THE most important question, and we take it extremely seriously.

TRAUMA-INFORMED DESIGN IS FOUNDATIONAL

We're developing this platform in partnership with the VA National Center for PTSD.

Safety Protocols:

  • 1. Mandatory Pre-Screening: PCL-5 (PTSD Checklist) + TBI questionnaire.
  • 2. Non-VR Alternatives: ALL content available in standard video format.
  • 3. Instant Escape: One-button exit from any immersive experience.
  • 4. Grounding Techniques: Built into sessions.
  • 5. Crisis Access: Veterans Crisis Line (988 then 1) prominently displayed.

Current Evidence Base:

  • * VA already uses VR exposure therapy for PTSD—proven effective in multiple RCTs.
  • * VR used successfully for pain management, surgical training, phobia treatment.
  • * Research shows VR increases engagement, retention, and skill-building.

Our 3-Phase Validation Pathway:

  • Phase I: Feasibility & acceptability testing
  • Phase II: Randomized controlled trial at 3-5 VA/DOD sites
  • Phase III: Multi-site effectiveness trial

NO. This supplements, not replaces.

INTEGRATION, NOT REPLACEMENT

Our platform works alongside existing VA peer support services, telehealth programs, and outpatient care.

  • * Extends Reach: Serves populations current programs can't reach.
  • * Fills Gaps: Provides 24/7 access between appointments.
  • * Reduces No-Shows: Eliminates transportation barriers.
SECURITY

Security & Privacy

Data protection and compliance

GOVERNMENT-GRADE SECURITY

Our infrastructure meets or exceeds all federal health data protection requirements.

Compliance Standards:

  • * HIPAA: Full compliance with Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act.
  • * FISMA: Federal Information Security Management Act requirements.
  • * FedRAMP Moderate: Cloud infrastructure certified to FedRAMP standards.

Technical Security:

  • * End-to-End Encryption (transit and at rest)
  • * CAC/PIV Authentication compatible
  • * Complete audit trails
  • * Regular third-party security audits

VA/DOD retains full ownership and control of all patient data. We never sell, share, or use data for advertising.

CLEARANCE-SAFE DESIGN

Platform designed specifically to allow service members with security clearances to seek help privately.

  • * Private Access: Anonymous usage option available.
  • * DOD-Approved Infrastructure: Meets security requirements for cleared personnel.
  • * Early Intervention: Seeking help before problems escalate actually protects clearances.

Note: Seeking treatment for SUD is generally NOT clearance-disqualifying—hiding problems IS.
