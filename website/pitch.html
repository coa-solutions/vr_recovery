RISK MANAGEMENT

We've Thought Through the Risks

VR triggers PTSD/TBI

Mitigation: Trauma-informed design with VA PTSD Center; mandatory screening; non-VR alternatives; safety monitoring

Low veteran adoption

Mitigation: Co-design with veterans; peer specialist promotion; VA provider referrals; military cultural competence

Difficult VA partnerships

Mitigation: Start with individual champions; demonstrate quick wins; leverage VA innovation programs

Technology barriers

Mitigation: Multi-device support; phone assistance; family training; simple interface

BIGGEST RISK WE'RE NOT TAKING:

Building on commercial VR platforms (Meta, etc.) that won't meet federal security requirements.

We designed for compliance first, features second.
