THE CRISIS

The Access Crisis

Veterans and service members face substance use disorders at 2-3x higher rates than civilians—yet only 10-15% receive treatment.

Logistical Barriers

  • ▸Rural veterans 40+ miles from VA facilities
  • ▸Deployed service members on ships/bases
  • ▸Transportation, childcare, employment conflicts

Cultural Barriers

  • ▸Military stigma around seeking help
  • ▸Fear of career impact
  • ▸Limited military-culturally competent providers

Financial Barriers

  • ▸Residential treatment: $10K-30K/month
  • ▸Long VA waitlists (30-90+ days)
  • ▸Limited TRICARE providers

Infantry, Fort Wainwright, Alaska

⚠ Challenge:
